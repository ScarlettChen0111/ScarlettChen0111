Essence Of Consciousness











A music visualization inspired by my contemplation of the question: What is the essence of human consciousness? If AI can be trained to talk, act, and even 'think' like a human, is it possible that humans themselves can be considered a sort of 'device' designed by a higher existence, and all information processed in our brain are just illusions?

Medium: After Effects, Cinema 4D
