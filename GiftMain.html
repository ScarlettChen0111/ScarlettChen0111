Gift From My Invisible Twin





Introduction


Gift From My Invisible Twin is a graduation gift for myself. This project consists of a self-help and self-motivational kit, and a video demonstrating the way I get along with my invisible twin(the other personality of mine).

The kit is meant to help me to stay disciplined and give myself mental strength to deal with obstacles by turning motivational and mood-boosting words that I gather when I talk to myself into text art, and help me to be more aware of my mind by visualizing the most frequent thoughts happening in my mind.

The video is meant to help people better understand how my invisible twin(the other personality of mine) helps me, and where the text content of kit comes from.



Significance


To me, this project helps me to keep my life on track when there’s no reliable external support.

To other audiences, this project is meant to provide them an example that shows the possibility that even when things become really bad at some point, one can be his or her own power source, and how one’s negative experiences, thoughts and emotions can be utilized.



Part 1: Self-Motivational Kit





Kit-pt1: Morning Boost Cards





Morning Boost Cards are a collection of 3D printed cards painted in black and pink that has a sentence that gives me a boost in the morning. The idea behind the cards is to create a ritual using a stack of black and pink cards that I can mix together randomly, and each night I will choose one but not read it, then I will place it beneath my pillow and read it until the next morning.

This ritual is meant to serve two purposes:

First, I will go to sleep knowing that the minute I awake, encouraging words will greet me.

Second, the motivational text will allow me to start my day with intense impulses, which keep myself from inertia. I am creating my own system of prayer. This way, the cards and the texts on it will work as a gift sent by the version of me from yesterday.







Kit-pt2: Errant Thoughts Gatherer





Errant Thoughts Gatherer is an LCD screen packed in pink and purple case that displays the most common errant thoughts in my mind. This device serves one single purpose: to present my most frequent errant thoughts and casual conversation contents of me talking to myself.

Since my errant thoughts were usually about a few topics, with which the conversations are related to, the thoughts were typed in the device before use. By doing this, I was able to check it anytime and anywhere, and when I checked it, it presented in front of my eyes, instead of going everywhere inside my mind, mixing with other important things that I need to pay attention to. It helps me to put things off my chest and stay focused.







Part 2: Story Narrated By My Invisible Twin

Story Narrated By My Invisible Twin is a video that demonstrates how I interact with the other “me” that I’ve been talking to.

Since he video is meant to help people better understand how my invisible twin(the other personality of mine) helps me, and where the text content of the other two parts comes from, the story is narrated in her tone instead of mine. Using her tone better demonstrates why she is born with me, and why I prefer to talk to her rather than other people. Using a third person’s voice to tell the life story also helps people to see why I believe what I believe now, and makes the tone less egoistic. Additionally, narrating the story from anther perspective adds more depth to the project as well.

The video incorporates a lot of mirrored clips of my daily activities, such as going to bed, doing makeup, etc, which are meant to convey the feeling of living with another personality.
