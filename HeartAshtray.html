Heart Ashtray/Incense Burner



Project Brief

3D modeled and printed heart ashtray/incense burner. The model was modeled in Blender and printed by Shapeways.

10.08 x 4.29 x 14.19 cm

As age advances, the feeling of the passing of time becomes more and more intense. Meanwhile, as time passes, our lives are stained by what we experience, consume, and how we respond. Our lives are just like this ashtray/incense burner. When we first receive it, it's clean and shiny. Once it's put to use, it starts to accumulate stains. As time passes, these stains become increasingly difficult to clean. Even though we can clean it frequently, no matter how carefully we do so, it will never look the same as when it first arrived in our home.

The ashtray is meant to reflect the stains we have in our own lives and remind people to value life and detoxify negative energies. When people use it as an ashtray, it leaves stains on both the ashtray and inside the smoker's body. After using it a few times, the cigarette stains on the ashtray reflect the harm it causes to the smoker's life. At the same time, if people use it as an incense burner, it can be seen as a positive approach to making one's life comfortable, since incense is frequently used in meditation and symbolizes calmness and healing. From my experience, incense stains are much easier to clean than cigarette stains.

No matter what we do in our lives—positive or negative—it will always have stains in the end. However, we can choose our own approach to handling it, and we always have the option of cleaning it whenever we want.








Materials


Aluminum casting: 3D modeled with Rhinoceros, printed with resin. Made an OOMOO mold and poured wax to make a wax model. Then casted with liquid Aluminum with lost-wax technique.

The aluminum casting was also chosen to be the exhibition cover for "Digital Crafting Basics". On view through January 2 - January 23, 2018, at Flaxman Library Main Collections. Source image




Porcelain: Printed and manufactured using Shapeways service. Porcelain is no longer available on their website now but you can still get it with metal materials.
