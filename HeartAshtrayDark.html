Heart Ashtray/Incense Burner



Project Brief


3D modeled and printed heart ashtray/incense burner. The model was modeled in Blender and printed by Shapeways.

10.08 x 4.29 x 14.19 cm As we age, the awareness of time's passage intensifies. Simultaneously, our lives become marked by our experiences, consumption habits, and responses.

Our lives mirror this ashtray or incense burner: pristine upon arrival, yet gradually accumulating stains with use. Despite regular cleaning, it never regains its original luster.

This object symbolizes the stains in our lives, urging us to cherish life and rid ourselves of negativity. Whether used for smoking or burning incense, it reflects both harm and healing.

Regardless of our actions, our lives inevitably gather stains. Yet, we retain the power to choose how to manage them, always capable of cleansing whenever we desire.








Materials



Aluminum casting: 3D modeled with Rhinoceros, printed with resin. Made an OOMOO mold and poured wax to make a wax model. Then casted with liquid Aluminum with lost-wax technique.

The aluminum casting was also chosen to be the exhibition cover for "Digital Crafting Basics". On view through January 2 - January 23, 2018, at Flaxman Library Main Collections. Source image




Porcelain: Printed and manufactured using Shapeways service. Porcelain is no longer available on their website now but you can still get it with metal materials.
