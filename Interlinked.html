INTERLINKED


Overview


In today's world, our existence is increasingly defined by the symbols we create. Individualism is on the rise, yet the internet binds us closer than ever before. This blend of rising individualism and intricate connections presents challenges, but also offers opportunities to pursue desired lifestyles. Inspired by Alexander McQueen's skull series, I have infused refinement into my designs, merging it with the concepts of 'connection' and 'interlinking' to create an accessory collection reflecting my appreciation for this new era.
