Metaphors

1. Matryoshka = high dimensional space; highly advanced intelligent life form

2. PRISM = symbol of Law of Physics in the current 3D world

3. Skull = human; human behavior and cognitive mode

4. Columns = abstract fences preventing human from escaping current cognition

We, humans, are moving and thinking in a mind-box. The box is the temporal, materialistic space where our mind and behavior are formed and limited by its physical base. We can still choose to make our lives colorful in this limited space. The only thing is that we need to remember: in the vastness of the universe, we are just some ephemeral organic matter. Our existence is transient.
