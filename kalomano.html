Kalomano Mask Poster



Kalomano Mask Poster

Project Brief

This poster was made for a professional mask company, Kalomano Inc. in 2021.

Kalomano was holding a sales event in 2021 winter, Shenzhen, China. This poster was designed for the sales event and was displayed in front of their WeWork office in Shenzhen. Along with the poster, we also designed and printed A4 leaflets to give out to people passing our office.



A4 Leaflet




What I Did

This is a project based experience. The only requirements for me were to use the company's existing logo and fonts, and the color theme should match their official website. Other than that, I was in charge of the whole design process.

When I was communicating with the merchant, he left me an impression of an easygoing and reasonable person. Since this poster was meant for a sales event, and the merchant would be staying in front of the office and introducing the masks to people, I offered him a suggestion that we could incorporate his own image in the poster. This is meant for eye-catching effect. The office was located in a plaza, and there were a lot of merchants posting their advertisements in the same space. As a result, while showing our professionalism, we also needed something that was able to grab more attention.

The merchant agreed with my proposal. The final option that we came to an agreement was to use his picture, in which he held his ID card. I transformed the ID card into a coupon, which showed the amount of discount offered in the event. When the poster was put to use, it did achieve what we expected. Quite a few people stopped by because they saw the small portrait on the poster. Most people who stopped by started the conversation by asking the merchant "is that you?", while pointing their fingers at the portrait.
