SaniTalk



Introduction

SaniTalk is a text-only app. The name of the app combines the word “sanitize” and “talk”. This app works as a text version of face-to-face talk—the only information exchanging medium is language (text), and the information leaves absolutely no trace on earth except in the user’s mind. Common functions in most communication apps such as audio, online calling, and image/file transferring are eliminated.

The application features point-to-point messaging, “burn after read”, monochrome interface which resembles golden ratio. The design philosophy behind SaniTalk is to create a communication tool that works as user’s most reliable messenger. It intends to make the communication process as simple and safe as possible by eliminating all superfluous features which can potentially jeopardize user’s privacy
