SaniTalk



Introduction & Significance





Introduction

SaniTalk is a text-only app. The name of the app combines the word “sanitize” and “talk”. This app works as a text version of face-to-face talk—the only information exchanging medium is language (text), and the information leaves absolutely no trace on earth except in the user’s mind. Common functions in most communication apps such as audio, online calling, and image/file transferring are eliminated.

The application features point-to-point messaging, “burn after read”, monochrome interface which resembles golden ratio. The design philosophy behind SaniTalk is to create a communication tool that works as user’s most reliable messenger. It intends to make the communication process as simple and safe as possible by eliminating all superfluous features which can potentially jeopardize user’s privacy



Significance

As technology develops, the traditional moral system is facing more and more challenges. What we consider ethical today may no longer be true in the future. Everyone has their secrets. These secrets need protection and may not necessarily be harmful, but they can bring potential risks to their owners if leaked. Nowadays, technology makes it tricky to protect confidential information. As a result, I designed this minimalist app that guards people’s privacy to the largest possible extent for those who have to communicate confidential information through digital devices.



Aesthetics





Color Palette, Icons, Fonts



The app has two color modes: LIGHTS ON and LIGHTS OFF. Color palettes are slightly different in these two modes. In order to keep the visuals harmonious, colors used in LIGHTS OFF mode are slightly darker than in LIGHTS ON mode. There are very few icons in SaniTalk. Most page links are identified with texts. The only three icons, from top to bottom, are “incoming text message”, “searching contact”, and “SaniTalk homepage”. The font used in SaniTalk is Avenir, a geometric, linear sans serif which is screen-friendly by being clean-cut and smooth. To keep the visuals constant without being plain and boring, three forms of Avenir-Avenir Medium, Avenir Book, and Avenir Light—are used in the app.




Aesthetics: Golden Ratio & Empty Rooms



SaniTalk’s background resembles either empty rooms or ambiguous spaces. The middle image is the texting room. Starting from the texting room, swiping right is the My Account page, which displays the right corner of an empty room; swiping left is the Contact List, which shows the left corner of an empty room.

The design concept is that when swiping right, the page goes right, which means the subject(user) is moving left. The motion can be seen as the user goes to the left room through the texting room wall, and where he arrives immediately is the right side of the destination, so the corner is on the right. Vice versa.

Simplicity and sophistication are the keywords of SaniTalk’s aesthetic, and all sophistications are hidden in details. The background images of SaniTalk are always monochrome and resemble the golden ratio. The corners of rooms are presented in the golden angle, which is approximately 137.5°. The empty rooms with Morandi colors are meant to work as quiet and safe spaces for users to text.



Functions





Point-To-Point Messaging & Intentional Inconvenience



Information communicated via SaniTalk will leave no trace on the planet except in the user’s mind. Messages sent between two users exist only in the two DEVICES used for texting, and exist only during the time set by the users. There will be no record on the server or in the app backstage, and texts will disappear once the time is up. Also, SaniTalk does not show the other user’s username in the chat room. This is a designed inconvenience, which guides the user to double check their selected partner’s serial number and username before entering the chat room. In this case, users can make sure their important messages are sent to the right person.




Burn After Read & Screenshot Reminder



To prevent texts from leaking, every single text will be permanently deleted after reading. Also, SaniTalk automatically sends an alert when the other user takes a screenshot of the message. The TEXT VANISHING TIME section in the settings is designed for users to set up how long their message will be displayed on their partner’s side. For instance, if I selected 20s to be my vanishing time and texted another user, the other user only has 20 seconds to read my message. If the time is too short for the other user, the other user has to communicate with me to set a longer time. This is a designed inconvenience as well. It’s meant to minimize the risk of the message being captured by other devices(e.g. being taken a picture by another cellphone).




Be Personal



In order to provide a more immersive experience of communicating in a personal space, SaniTalk allows users to set their own security questions. It distinguishes SaniTalk from most apps, which have fixed security questions that may not fit some users’ needs and the answers can be easily forgotten. Users can set customized questions that work best for them, which makes it easier for users to retrieve their password or account. Moreover, in order to adapt to different lighting environments and moods, SaniTalk has LIGHTS ON mode for brighter(e.g. under sunlight, daytime) and LIGHTS OFF mode for darker environments(e.g. in the bed or taxi at midnight).
